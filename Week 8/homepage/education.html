De La Salle Araneta University

High School Diploma

De La Salle Araneta University (DLSAU) is the seventh member of De La Salle System and the youngest Lasallian university in the Philippines. Established in 1946 as the Araneta Institute of Agriculture in Bulacan, the university was moved to Malabon in 1947 and renamed as the Gregorio Araneta University Foundation in 1978.


In 2002, the university became an official member of the De La Salle System—following a 15-year long process of integration into the system. With specializations in veterinary medicine, food technology, and agricultural sciences, the university maintains two campuses in Malabon and San Jose del Monte, Bulacan.

FEU Institute of Technology

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with Specialization in Software Engineering

FEU Institute of Technology, also referred to as FEU Tech, is a private, non-sectarian, coeducational higher education institution in Sampaloc, Manila, Philippines. It offers courses in the fields of Engineering and Computer Studies.
